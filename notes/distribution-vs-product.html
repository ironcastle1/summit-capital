Distribution beats product more often than founders realise

Most ecommerce founders believe they are in the product business.

In reality they are in the distribution business.

Two brands can sell nearly identical products. One stalls at £200k revenue while another scales past £20M.

The difference is rarely the product.

It is the system used to acquire customers.

Creative pipelines, paid traffic systems, and offer engineering now determine the ceiling of most ecommerce brands.

The brands that win treat distribution as the primary asset.
